Overview

Rice is arguably the most important staple food for nearly every Filipino household. However, the cost of rice is rapidly rising despite promises to lower grain prices.

We wanted to find out about the status of the rice supply in the Philippines and explore how it relates to the consumer price of rice.

Data

We obtained our data from OpenSTAT, an open data platform used by the Philippine Statistics Office (PSA) to share data. We found data on consumer price indexes, retail prices, as well as production and imports for rice in OpenSTAT.
